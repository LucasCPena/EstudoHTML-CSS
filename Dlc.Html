Original soundtrack composed for Spiritfarer that highlights all the memorable moments of the game. An emotionally moving and heartwarming orchestral score that accompanies Stella along her journey, as she cares for her spirit friends before guiding them to the afterlife.
Music & Lyrics by Max LL
Vocals in What Will You Leave Behind (End Title) Performed by Maude Plante-Husaruk
Vocals in What Will You Leave Behind (Reprise) Performed by Timbre Cierpke
Additional performances by Maude Plante-Husaruk (Vocals), Maria Grigoryeva (Violin) & Leo Bercoff (Harmonica)
Artwork by Jo-Annie Gauthier
Mastering by Harris Newman
Total duration: 1 hour and 17 minutes
